My present understanding of education in the IT perspective is that technology plays a vital role in transforming how people learn and teach. IT makes education more accessible and flexible through online platforms, digital resources, and virtual classrooms. It also helps learners develop essential digital skills like coding, data analysis, and cybersecurity. However, challenges such as the digital divide, over-reliance on technology, and privacy issues remain. Overall, IT in education offers great opportunities but requires responsible use to ensure equity and effectiveness.
